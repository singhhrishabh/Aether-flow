◈ LAYER 01 — PERCEPTION
The Eyes & Ears of Aether-Flow
360° Thermal Awareness
WHY THIS LAYER EXISTS
Cooling that reacts to what it cannot see will always be too late. This layer gives the AI complete situational awareness — temperature at 6 precise points, a full 768-pixel thermal image, humidity, power draw, and fluid flow — all simultaneously, every 500ms.
TMP117 ×6 — ±0.1°C Precision
Six sensors at chip center, memory, VRM, intake, exhaust, cold plate. Maps WHERE heat builds, not just how much.
MLX90640 — Thermal Camera
32×24 IR grid = 768 thermal pixels per frame. Sees hotspots forming across the chip surface before sensors react.
INA226 — Power Monitor ×4
Measures current draw per Peltier. Rising power predicts heat 2–3 seconds before temperature sensors register it.
SHT40 — Dew Point Guard
Humidity + ambient temp → dew point calculation. Prevents condensation on electronics absolutely.

◈ LAYER 03 — ACTUATION
Molecular-Level Heat Transfer
Solid-State · Silent · Precise
WHY THIS LAYER EXISTS
Intelligence is useless without the ability to act. Four independently controlled Peltier modules can cool specific zones of a chip differently. The microfluidic pump spreads cold uniformly. Together they deliver exactly what the AI prescribes — at millisecond precision.
TEC1-12706 Peltier ×4
Solid-state heat pumps. One side goes cold, other expels heat. Four independent zones give spatial cooling control — cool the CPU harder than memory.
Microfluidic Cold Plate
Custom aluminium with precision-milled microchannels. Coolant flows through and spreads cold uniformly across the entire chip contact surface.
Kamoer NKP Micropump
Variable-speed AI-controlled pump. Unlike fixed-speed pumps, it delivers exactly the right flow for each moment. Continuously variable from 0–100%.
IRF540N MOSFETs ×4
Electronic switches allowing 3.3V ESP32 logic to control 12V Peltier power. Switch 25,000 times per second — inaudible, precise.

◈ LAYER 04 — COMMUNICATION
Data Flows at Every Level
Sensors → Brain → Actuators
WHY THIS LAYER EXISTS
The sensors, brain, and actuators are physically separate components. This layer is the wiring that connects them — fast enough that no data is ever stale, simple enough that it never fails. Everything talks to everything in real time.
I2C Bus — GPIO 21/22
Two wires carry data from all sensors simultaneously. Each sensor has a unique address — ESP32 knows exactly who it's reading.
USB Serial — 115,200 baud
Bidirectional JSON pipeline between ESP32 and Pi. Telemetry flows up every 500ms. AI commands flow down every inference cycle.
PWM Signals — 25kHz
Four independent channels control Peltier modules, fans, and pump. Each gets a different duty cycle based on what the AI prescribes.
JSON Protocol
Human-readable structured data format. Every reading and every command is logged, debuggable, and parseable by the AI model.

◈ LAYER 06 — SAFETY
The System That Never Fails
Hardware-Level · Software-Independent
WHY THIS LAYER EXISTS
Electronics are expensive. Heat is dangerous. Every safety mechanism here is independent — hardware relay fires even if all software crashes. The Dew Guard is always on. PID fallback activates the moment AI goes silent. The system cannot fail dangerously.
Hardware PID Fallback
If AI silent for 2 seconds, ESP32 switches instantly to classical PID control. Cooling never stops — even if the Pi crashes completely.
Dew Point Guard
If cold plate approaches dew point within 5°C, Peltier power auto-reduces. Condensation on electronics is prevented absolutely.
Hardware Safety Relay
Physical relay cuts all Peltier power at >85°C or >60W. Entirely software-independent. Last line of defense that always works.
Thermal Runaway Detection
If temperature rises faster than 2°C/second — cooling loop failure detected — emergency stop fires before damage occurs.
